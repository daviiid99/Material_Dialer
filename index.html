Material Dialer - Privacy

Material Dialer is a personal contacts app replacement focused on user privacy
All data resides on user device and we don't catch any sensitive data externally

How Do We treat your Data?

Material Dialer have access to the following permissions on your device :

  1. External Storage (To backup and import your contacts)
  2. Phone Calls (To use the native phone app call screen)

Examples of permissions requested by Material Dialer


Play Store Proteccion


This app is signed and can only be upgraded using the Google Play Store

To check for updates go to
Settings>About Material Dialer>Check for Updates
If a new update is found, you'll be prompted to update it using the Google Play Store
